## Patterns Observed

- **CPQ blocks are killing closeable deals.** OPP-1004 and OPP-1017 are both in Negotiation with active buyers but legally cannot close. Process failure, not sales failure.
- **No executive sponsors on large deals.** OPP-1005 ($610K), OPP-1010 ($530K), and OPP-1015 ($380K) all lack a champion. Win-pattern data is unambiguous: deals above $200K without executive sponsorship do not close at forecast rate.
- **Above-guardrail discounts are widespread and ungoverned.** Five of seven deals carry discounts exceeding the 15% guardrail. None have triggered a formal Deal Desk review or VP-level exception approval. Discounting has not re-engaged stalled buyers — it has transferred pricing leverage with zero reciprocal commitment.
